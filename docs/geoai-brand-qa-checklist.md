# GeoAI Brand QA Checklist

Use this checklist before merging UI-facing GeoAI changes.

## Homepage

- Hero has maximum 2 key CTA buttons.
- Hero CTAs use approved primary/secondary styling.
- No `Explore use cases`-style third action appears in the primary hero CTA row unless explicitly requested.
- The hero still feels premium, light and investor-demo ready.

## Buttons And Actions

- Primary buttons use `brand` background with white text.
- Secondary buttons use white/surface background, `line` border and `ink` text.
- Button labels are short and explicit.
- Explanatory copy appears in helper text, subtitles or status strips, not inside long button labels.

## Screens

- No heavy dark UI has been introduced.
- No neon or random color palette appears.
- Cards use consistent border, radius and shadow treatment.
- Text does not overflow or clip in cards, buttons, sidebars or reports.
- Investor-facing screens avoid raw technical/debug wording.

## Maps

- Live Mapbox views remain functional where applicable.
- Fallback/static maps look intentional and demo-grade.
- Selected geometry is visible, credible and not cartoonish.
- District labels, selected-site labels and layer controls do not overlap badly.

## Dashboards And Reports

- Analysis and comparison dashboards preserve the light enterprise brand.
- Map Context and decision cards align cleanly where they are side by side.
- Report preview and printable reports use the same brand colors and evidence language.
- Demo/sample/offline source status is clearly labeled and never presented as official validation.

## Responsive

- 390px mobile has no horizontal overflow.
- 768px tablet stacks naturally.
- 1440px desktop preserves balanced card alignment and visible primary actions.
